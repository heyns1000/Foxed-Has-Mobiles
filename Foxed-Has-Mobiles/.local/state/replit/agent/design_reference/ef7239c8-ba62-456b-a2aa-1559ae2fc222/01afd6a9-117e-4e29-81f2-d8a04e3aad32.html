Competition Rules & Requirements

Guidelines for participating in the Fox Mascot Icon Competition

Technical Requirements

  • SVG format preferred, PNG acceptable (min 512x512px)
  • Maximum file size: 5MB
  • Original artwork only - no stock imagery
  • Scalable design for mobile interfaces

Design Guidelines

  • Fox-themed mascot design required
  • Mobile-friendly and dashboard compatible
  • Clear visibility at 32x32px minimum
  • Professional, modern aesthetic

Submission Process

  • Register account with valid email
  • Maximum 5 entries per participant
  • Include design brief (max 500 words)
  • Deadline: March 31, 2024

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity & Originality (30%)
  • Technical Excellence (25%)
  • Mobile Usability (25%)
  • Brand Alignment (20%)
